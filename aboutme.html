I have a cat, and her name is Hoshi. I adopted her in 2018 from a rescue shelter in Portland, OR, and we've beeen best buds ever since. You can usually find her snuggling in my lap while I try to work on the computer, or lounging in her cat tree staring at the birds and squirrels outside.

She loves stinky wet food, chasing lasers, chin scratches, and playing fetch (except she never brings the toy back -__-).

She is not a fan of closed doors, vacuum cleaners, or strangers.

I love strength training. I originally began with powerlifting in 2014, then about 2 years later I began learning Olympic weightlifting at a gym in Portland.

I took some time off from lifting while I finished up undergrad and moved back to California to begin working, and recently got back into it about a year ago. Aside from helping me stay healthy, I also love the community around the sport- I've made some of my closest friends through lifting.

I'm hoping to compete in my second competition sometime later this year!
